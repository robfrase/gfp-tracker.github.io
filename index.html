FOR GOLF COACHES

Data Worth Teaching

You watch your students play. You see the patterns. But their golf app shows you fairways hit and strokes gained. That data doesn't match what you see.

The Coaching Problem

A student with a 75 might have 8 process errors — or 22. Traditional apps can't tell the difference. They see the score, not the swing. You spend half the lesson translating between what the app says and what actually happened.

With GFP, You See:

  • Their process — not just their score
  • Strike quality — fat, thin, toe, heel
  • Mental patterns — fear, anger, focus loss
  • Pressure patterns — casual vs tournament

The Conversation Starter

Instead of:

"Your strokes gained on approach is -0.4"

You can say:

"You've logged Heavy contact on 6 of your last 9 approach shots. That's a pattern. Let's work on bottom-of-arc."
